Who We Are

Where Engineering Depth Meets Strategic Vision

Marafis is a specialist technology consultancy founded on a single conviction: that exceptional software and architecture decisions are the highest-leverage investment a business can make. We bring over two decades of hands-on engineering and technical leadership to every engagement.

From greenfield platforms to complex legacy transformations — across finance, insurance, e-commerce, and telecoms — we've led the initiatives that matter most.

Architecture-first thinking. We design before we build — ensuring every decision is intentional, documented, and aligned to your business goals.

Hands-on delivery. We don't hand off slide decks. Our consultants write code, lead teams, and stay accountable through delivery.

Outcome-oriented partnership. We measure our success by yours — reduced time-to-market, lower operational risk, and platforms that last.
